Our story

Skincare you can read.

We started Hemlock & Hare in 2019 because the formula on the back of every bottle we loved had forty-three ingredients and we couldn't pronounce thirty-five of them.

Every product is built from eight or fewer cold-pressed botanicals. Each batch is made in a converted dairy barn in Rhinebeck and arrives at your door inside two weeks of being made. Nothing sits on a shelf for nine months pretending to be fresh.

— Margot Halsey Founder · Formulator
