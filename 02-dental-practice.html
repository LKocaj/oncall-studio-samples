Our process

Meet Dr. Rooks

Twelve years of careful work.

Dr. Mira Rooks founded Rook & Well in 2014 after completing her DDS at the New York University College of Dentistry. Her training brought her to the lab as often as to the chair — every veneer and crown leaving her practice is finished by hand on-site.
