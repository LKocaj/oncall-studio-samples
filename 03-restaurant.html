The kitchen

A trattoria, run like one.

We opened Via dell'Orto in 2019 to cook the food we grew up eating in Trastevere. Most of the menu hasn't moved since. The pasta is still rolled by hand at 4pm. The amari are still poured by Marcello, who runs the bar like a Roman side street.

We don't take more than two seatings a night, and we don't take phone reservations. Resy at midnight, thirty days out. Walk in at the bar before 6 and you'll usually get a seat.

— Chef Antonio Forte Co-owner · Chef de cuisine
