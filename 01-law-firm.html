§ 05 · The desk

How a deal moves through the firm.

Every engagement runs through the same five-phase model — refined over 300+ transactions and tuned to give principals visibility at every gate.

Standard transaction architecture

01 · Origination & conflictsEngagement letter, conflicts clearance, deal team staffing D-30 to D-21
02 · Diligence & structureData room, structuring memos, tax and regulatory framing D-21 to D-7
03 · NegotiationTerm sheet, definitives, ancillary agreements, debt commitment papers D-7 to D-1
04 · Signing & antitrustHSR, foreign filings, regulatory pre-clearance, financing close D-0 to D+45
05 · Closing & integrationFunds flow, escrow, post-closing covenants, transition counsel D+45 to D+90
